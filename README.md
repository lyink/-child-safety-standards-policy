Child Safety Standards Policy
Mapenzichat Application
1. Introduction

Mapenzichat is committed to protecting children and preventing child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE). We enforce strict safety measures to ensure our platform remains safe and respectful for all users.

Mapenzichat maintains a zero-tolerance policy against any form of child sexual exploitation, abuse, or harmful behavior involving minors.

2. Age Restrictions

Mapenzichat is strictly intended for users aged 18 years and above. Individuals under the age of 18 are not allowed to create accounts or use the application.

Mapenzichat reserves the right to suspend, restrict, or permanently remove accounts suspected of violating age requirements.

3. Prohibited Content and Behaviour

Mapenzichat strictly prohibits the following activities:

Child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE)

Child sexual abuse material (CSAM)

Grooming or manipulation of minors

Sharing, uploading, or distributing inappropriate or exploitative content involving minors

Impersonating minors

Any sexual or exploitative interaction involving children

Users found violating these rules will be permanently banned and may be reported to appropriate authorities.

4. Reporting and User Protection

Mapenzichat provides in-app mechanisms allowing users to report:

Inappropriate messages or content

Suspicious or harmful behaviour

Harassment or abuse

Child safety concerns

All reports are reviewed promptly by our moderation team. Actions taken may include:

Removal of violating content

Suspension or permanent banning of user accounts

Escalation of serious cases to relevant authorities

5. Content Moderation

Mapenzichat actively monitors user-generated content to ensure compliance with safety standards. Content that violates our policies will be removed immediately after detection or user reporting.

We continuously improve our moderation systems to maintain a safe environment for users.

6. Compliance with Child Safety Laws

Mapenzichat complies with applicable child protection laws and regulations. When required, Mapenzichat cooperates fully with regional and national law enforcement authorities regarding child safety investigations.
